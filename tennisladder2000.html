NOTES

  • SEE YOU NEXT YEAR: The ladder season ended 31 October 2000. Next year's season begins 15 April 2001.
  • TOP 10: The history of the top 10 players on the ladder is posted at http://rsd.gsfc.nasa.gov/tennisclub/tennisladder.top10.html .
  • NETS UP: The nets on the western Har-Tru courts have been taken up at the bottom to allow the leaves to blow under so that the leaves end up in an easily raked location, out of the way, near the fences. Please don't cut the ties holding the nets up, unless you volunteer to rake the leaves.
  • WINTER PLAY: The Har-Tru courts will be gradually shut down in November. There should be two to four useable clay courts until Thanksgiving, but without the benefit of the sprinklers. The hard courts will remain open year-round.
  • WINTER FACILITIES
    • The Paint Branch Tennis Development facility offers memberships, in addition to hourly court time.
    • The Fairland tennis bubble has collapsed. It should be replaced in November.
    • The University of Maryland tennis bubble is up and running, with spot time and full-season subscriptions available at modest rates.
