NOTES

  • THE LADDER IN WINTER: Halloween was the last day of the 2006 ladder season. The 2007 season opens April 15.
  • WINTER CLOSINGS: The drinking fountains and courts 5-8 will close when the weather goes bad in November. The sprinklers and courts 1-4 will close by Thanksgiving, with the coming of overnight freezes. The lighted hard courts will remain open all winter; the lights are in excellent condition.
  • FALL SINGLES TOURNAMENT: The GTC 2006 fall singles tournament was played on 23-24 September. In the final, John Cannizzo def. Tom Weisz 7-6, 7-5.
