Members can withdraw from the ladder and go "on reserve" at any time for any reason. They can return anytime during the year with a 5-rung penalty. At the end of the season, "reserve" players are returned to the ladder with a 5-rung penalty.

NOTES

  • DOWN FOR THE WINTER: The 2008 ladder season ended October 31. The HarTru courts will remain open, without water, until around Thanksgiving. They will re-open when the weather permits, typically late March. The 2009 ladder season starts April 15.
  • LABOR DAY SINGLES TOURNAMENT: John Cannizzo defeated Cliff Liu in the finals of the 2008 Labor Day singles tournament in a third set super-tiebraker, 3-6, 7-5, (10-8).
  • MEMORIAL SINGLES TOURNAMENT: Cliff Liu defeated John Cannizzo in the finals of the 2008 Memorial Day singles tournament in a third set super-tiebraker, 2-6, 6-2, (11-9).
  • DROWNED OAKS: In 2007, the Rec Center paved the back road and parking lot, creating a swamp with no drainage, which drowned dozens of oak trees. In July 2008, the Rec Center re-installed the drain pipe that had been under the road to the south of the swamp, solving the problem.
