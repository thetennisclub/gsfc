NOTES

  • MORE PARKING, LESS ACCESS: In November 2005, GEWA decided to make overflow parking for the Rec Center by expanding the GTC back parking lot, doubling the width of the back road and the number of parking spaces. However, that also means enclosing the GTC courts within the Rec Center fence. During big Rec Center events, members' access will be through the Good Luck Road gate.
  • SOIL CONSERVATION: In 2006, GSFC is re-routing Soil Conservation Road around the north side of the Rec Center, so we won't have to go through the security gates to get to the courts from the main campus in 2007.
  • LADDER RESERVATIONS: At the 31 March 2005 Club meeting, the members voted to stop requiring court reservations as a condition for ladder membership next year. That will make ladder membership "free" in 2006.
  • REWARD FOR LADDER ACTIVITY: For 2006, the ladder chairman proposes to encourage more ladder play by promoting the active ladder members, i.e. those who played a ladder match during a month, to the top of the ladder at the end of each good-weather month (at the end of May, June, July, August, and September).
