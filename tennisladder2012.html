NOTES

  • SINGLES WIN -- The Labor Day Weekend 2012 singles tournament was won by John Cannizzo, who defeated Tom Weisz in the final (6-3, 6-0).
  • TOURNAMENT PROMOTION: Ladder members playing in the weekend singles tournament are promoted ahead of members who didn't play, in the order that they finished in the tournament, if they played all rounds. The promoted are open to unrestricted challenges for the following month. The ladder promotions after Labor Day are: Cannizzo(4-0), Koeppl(3-1), Negri(2-2), Pence(1-3), and Calloway(1-3).
  • RESURFACING -- By mid-May, Har-Tru courts 1 and 3-6 were resurfaced with 3 and 1 ton/court of fresh clay, respectively. At the end of August, court 2 was resurfaced with 3 tons of clay, and is half done with a fresh set of singles lines and every-other nail (eventually, 1924 nails in 481 feet of taut plastic tape).
