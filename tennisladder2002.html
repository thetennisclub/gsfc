NOTES

  • CLOSING NOTE: In the last match of the 2002 ladder season, Cannizzo(2) held Markus(4): 7-6, 6-7, 7-6 (whew!).
  • THE END IS HERE: The ladder season ended on October 31. Next year's ladder season begins April 15th.
  • USE 'EM OR LOSE 'EM: The unrestricted challenges granted to this year's new players expired on October 31.
  • NEW WALL: The big old plywood practice wall has been taken down and replaced by a smaller tough new fiberglass wall filled with sand. The old wall was coming part at the seams. The new wall is tipped to bounce the ball back farther, and curved to return the ball back closer to the centerline.
  • COURT STATUS: There are still many irregular spots on courts 1-4 that we hope will settle down during the winter. The bottom of the nets have been clipped up to allow the wind to blow the leaves under them. We purchased a large drag-rake to make it easier to keep the courts clean. The clay courts will remain open until late November, but the daily watering will stop with the overnight frost/freezes. The drinking fountains have been turned off because the fountain between courts 3 & 4 sprang a leak, again.
