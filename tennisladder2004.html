NOTES

  • SPRING FORWARD: The 2005 ladder will begin on 15 April 2005. Challenges are unrestricted if played by May 15. Dennis Chesters stays on as ladder chairman.
  • COURTS: The HarTru courts are all open for play. We will have a line-party some Saturday morning to replace 6 baselines, when the weather gets nicer in May. Dennis Chesters will send out a call.
  • MAINTENANCE: The grounds keeper, Larry Walls, retired in September 2004. He will continue court maintenance this spring, after which we must find a replacement.
  • OFF-HOLIDAY TOURNAMENTS: The singles tournaments this year may be held the week after the Memorial Day and Labor Day holidays, to accomodate vactioners, including the tournament chairman, Andy Negri.
  • BALL MACHINE: The Club will buy a new ball machine this spring, to replace the old, jamming box.
  • LADDER RESERVATIONS: At the 31 March 2005 Club meeting, the members voted to stop requiring court reservations as a condition for ladder membership next year, 2006.
